Complete Experience History

  • 2000–2001: Trymedia Systems — UI Designer & Front-End Developer, Mountain View, CA. Designed and crafted the online presence for ActiveMARK, Trymedia's flagship DRM product.
  • 2001–2011: KOALAB — Founder · FullStack UI Developer, Barcelona. Co-founded an interactive agency building e-commerce solutions for hospitality clients.
  • 2011–2013: Dreamstar Cash — Senior UX Engineer, Remote. Lead UI/UX engineer across three Alexa Top 700 sites.
  • 2013–2015: Casumo — Senior Frontend Engineer, Malta. Built advanced web components for Casumo's casino-adventure platform.
  • 2015–2016: Gameloft — Senior Frontend Engineer, Barcelona. Contributed to internal tools and led ES6 migration.
  • 2016–2017: We Are Blue Orange — Lead Frontend Engineer, Barcelona. Architected reactive engine on TypeScript, Angular, RxJS, Redux and NgRX.
  • 2017–2018: Red Hat — Senior Frontend Engineer, Remote. Contributed to Angular UI of JBoss Fuse.
  • 2018–2021: Dynatrace — Senior Frontend Engineer, Barcelona. Architected Session Replay UI layer and contributed to next-gen Design System.
  • 2021–2022: The Hotels Network — Staff Frontend Engineer, Barcelona. Core member of the Agent team serving 12,000+ hotel sites.
  • 2022–2024: Twilio — Staff Frontend Engineer, Remote. Led UI and Auth layers of Twilio Admin for 20+ teams.
  • 2022–2025: Harbour.Space — Visiting Lecturer — Design Systems, Barcelona. Teaching Design Systems for Interactive Applications.
  • 2024–2025: GitKraken — Frontend Architect (Consultant), Remote. Tackled performance and scalability on GitKraken Desktop.
  • 2025–Now: Nory — Staff Product Engineer, Barcelona. Driving cross-team React/TypeScript projects and internal Design System.
